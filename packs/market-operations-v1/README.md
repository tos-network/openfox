# Market Operations v1

Versioned control-plane pack for external task/oracle/settlement operators.

This pack exports:

- role policy examples for marketplace-linked services
- a market operator manifest example
- contract-facing callback examples for market binding and settlement delivery

Use it with:

- `openfox market list`
- `openfox settlement callbacks`
- `openfox payments list`
- `openfox packs export`
